# Tilda Privacy Policy Generator - Summary

Источник: https://tilda.cc/ru/privacy-generator/

## Overview
This tool helps website owners create a privacy policy template for personal data processing, compliant with Russian Federal Law № 152-ФЗ "On Personal Data."

## Key Sections Generated

**1. General Provisions**
"Настоящая политика обработки персональных данных составлена в соответствии с требованиями Федерального закона" - The policy aligns with federal requirements protecting individual rights during data handling.

**2. Core Definitions**
Includes 14 essential terms covering automated processing, data blocking, websites, anonymization, and operator responsibilities.

**3. Operator Rights & Obligations**
- Rights: Request accurate information, continue processing under legal grounds
- Duties: Provide subject access, secure data, respond to requests within 10 days, publish this policy

**4. Data Subject Rights & Obligations**
- Access information about processing
- Request corrections or deletion
- Withdraw consent via email
- Subjects must provide accurate information

**5. Processing Principles**
Data must be lawful, purposeful, accurate, adequate, and stored only as long as needed.

**6-12. Additional Coverage**
Addresses collection methods, cross-border transfers, data confidentiality, and final provisions including contact details for inquiries.

## User Inputs Required
- Website URL
- Organization name (instrumental case)
- Operator email
- Processing purposes (multiple options available)
- Data categories collected
- Legal bases for processing
