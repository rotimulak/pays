# Privacy Policy Template - Конструктор 2025

Источник: https://politika-konfidencialnost.ru

## Key Sections Included:

**1. General Provisions** - References Federal Law 152-FZ "On Personal Data" and establishes the operator's commitment to protecting user rights.

**2. Core Definitions** - Covers 14 essential terms including "automated processing," "data subject," "operator," and "personal data."

**3. Operator Rights & Obligations** - Lists permissions (obtaining accurate information, continuing processing under specified conditions) and duties (providing data access information, implementing security measures, publishing the policy).

**4. Data Subject Rights & Obligations** - Users can request information about their data, demand corrections, withdraw consent, and lodge complaints. They must provide accurate information.

**5. Processing Principles** - Emphasizes lawful basis, purpose limitation, data minimization, accuracy, and appropriate retention periods.

**6. Processing Conditions** - Identifies seven valid bases for data processing, including user consent and contractual necessity.

**7. Collection, Storage & Transfer Procedures** - Details security implementation, third-party restrictions, and data update processes via privacy@site.ru.

**8. Permitted Actions** - Specifies collection, storage, updating, deletion, and automated processing activities.

**9-11. Final Sections** - Address cross-border transfers, confidentiality obligations, and amendment procedures.

## Additional Resources Provided:

The page includes FAQ sections explaining what constitutes personal data, placement recommendations, penalties for non-compliance (up to 75,000 rubles), and technical requirements including Russian server hosting mandates.
